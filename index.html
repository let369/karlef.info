The dose of inspiration you require

A first attempt to connect inspire and motivate

Welcome and thank you for visiting my personal page fellow wanderers. Not all those who wander are lost as a good friend of mine says. It's just that sometimes we require an extra push, a vibe of positivity and exhortation to try and start something new.

What's the meaning of all this you ask ?

It is important to live a life full of happiness because in the end that’s what really matters. As we only live once we have to experience as many things as possible. One could relate living a happy life as living a successful life. This sets your every day goal to live your life to the fullest while you can and fill your day with things you truly enjoy. Of course I don’t have a golden rule to share on what to do in order to be happy and successful. This is something subjective and up to you to find out. My aim is to motivate you to start something that you have been procrastinating for a long time. The satisfaction I got when I stopped procrastinating myself urged me to do so. Take control of your life and make every day meaningful.

Life isn't about finding yourself.

Sometimes however as thinking human beings we are troubled. As a result we find ourselves in situations questioning every decision and action we take. This is not necessarily bad unless we are troubled with wrong questions. Even though the previous statement is subjective I refer to questions and decisions that will affect your personal growth and lead you towards happiness and success.

At some point in our life we have to decide what do we really like that could be our future profession. I bet you have heard the quote “Do what you love and you will never work a day in your life” many times and well its true. Of course it could be and would make sense if it's something related to your studies. However it’s a privilege that in our days there is a broad spectrum of careers. Most important is the opportunity of turning your hobby into your profession. It’s wonderful and advantageous if you find your interest early. Throughout all this journey, though, it’s really important to present characteristics that consist the solid base of your character as a person. Be more humane and build upon that your distinctive values. Being happy, positive and optimistic backed up with characteristics like modesty and gratefulness will establish a strong self-image. Especially I want to point out being grateful because it’s a fact that as a generation we take so many things for granted that others only dream of. Think about that the next time you drink a glass of water, take a walk and hopefully you will get my point. Another thing to keep in mind is that nobody is perfect. We all have flaws and imperfections that are part of our character. However, with sufficient self knowledge and respectfulness to the others none of that should be an obstacle in your path.

This could be your formula that you enhance with determination, uprightness, impartiality, selflessness and persistence. In my opinion another significant characteristic the majority lacks nowadays is to be more expressive. Do not hesitate to speak out your mind for two main reasons. Firstly you state your motives over and over again to yourself and your circle and second you remain focused on your target. Society is a complex, dynamic system, where nothing is permanent and absolute. So it’s a matter of mindset till you realise, that you can have an impact on it and make a change.

Key characteristics to keep in mind

Passion

Try to find as soon as possible what you are passionate for. From that point the roadmap will be straight and clear.

Integrity

Always remind to yourself why you started. Let nothing corrupt or divert you from your cause.

Patience

Patience is a virtue. Great and successful achievements require time and even reattempt.

Optimism

Always be positive and keep moving forward. Have trust in yourself and your actions.

Ambition

Keep your goals and expectations high. Strive for the best and don’t compromise.

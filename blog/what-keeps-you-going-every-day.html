The dose of inspiration you require

What keeps you going every day?

Some people have what it takes to remain positive and productive while pursuing a goal. Others require a bit of extra help.

06-June-2019

All of us, I believe, have goals that we pursue daily. Or maybe a passion that keeps us busy in our free time. In any case it is important to remain focused on what you are trying to achieve. Once you define your long term objectives, you have to determine your next actions, that will bring you one step closer to your goal. Throughout this course one of the challenges is to remain mentally strong and stable.

First and for most, doing an 8 hour work can be tiring and most importantly time consuming. In addition other daily responsibilities leave us with even less personal time. As a result this could easily demotivate a person from any productive activity. Alongside, we are constantly influenced by all kind of distractions. The distractions can vary, however social media dominate in this area undoubtedly. So it is a personal responsibility to make this realisation and take measures before you get out off track.

Another interesting aspect worth mentioning is how society in our days influences people. Personally I believe society has ingrained negative characteristics in the nature of humans. And all that because of the promotion and rise of egocentrism, dishonesty and jealousy. More and more frequently we encounter negative and malicious people. And it is sad because their motive is dragging others down instead of aiming higher for themselves.

As a result I started thinking, how can I keep this influence away from me. How can I strengthen the wall of positivity and determination around me. The idea I came up with was a decorative project, quite simple and easy to complete.

Starting an upbeat day and maintaining a positive attitude through out it, could become quite hard. So I decided to surround myself, both literally and figuratively, with positivity and motivation.

The idea is so simple that anybody can copy it and adjust it to personal needs. All you require is a couple text frames that you can print and your favourite quotes that inspire you. I wanted to create the frames myself so I had to learn the basics of working with Illustrator. So another positive thing came out of this and a new skill learned.

Once I got 4-5 frame variations, I started gathering the quotes that I would use. Two great websites to search are goodreads and BrainyQuote. Goodreads is also an awesome place to find great books based on your interests and reviews from other readers. Next step is to “frame” your quotes. You can get as creative as you want with fonts, colours, frames etc. Once this step is complete you can start printing your end results and cutting out shapes. Final step is sticking these banners any place you like.

Now you are surrounded by something you made yourself and it works in your benefit. You keep reminding yourself why you wake up everyday and what you are working towards to. Also its quite flexible as at any certain moment you can change some or all of them based on your mood. I think it is something simple that anyone can do and hopefully it will have a positive impact as intended. It would be great if any of you attempt it and let me know how did it go.

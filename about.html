Here goes a short personal introduction. I will try to keep it interesting and pinpoint the landmarks of the course. So I was born and raised in a small city in the north east Greece called Xanthi. There is no much that it can offer from career perspective and general opportunities but it definitely has a special place in my heart.

In 2013 I moved to Thessaloniki for my studies in the faculty of Applied Informatics in the University of Macedonia. During my student years I started traveling independently around Europe. These lead to be one of the main reasons to arouse my interest in moving abroad. The first initiative was during my 3rd year of studies when I decided to apply for short term abroad studies through the Erasmus program. My selected destination was Groningen, the Netherlands, where I moved in February of 2017. While I was abroad and close to graduating I thought, why not seize the opportunity and stay. After applications, calls and interviews I was accepted as an intern software engineer at Bloomreach (ex Hippo). So, after 6 incredible months that I could easily characterise as best time of my life, I had to move to Amsterdam and start this new challenge.

While working and enjoying life in Amsterdam I started working on my bachelor thesis with subject “Sentiment analysis on twitter posts with use of Elastic stack”. In April 2018 I officially graduated and after that received the next offer to remain now as a junior software engineer. During my so far experience at Bloomreach I have worked on multiple projects both internal and external for clients like American Express, Ahold Delhaize, Topdanmark, International Card Services and others. For more work related information please check out the portfolio section.

It is important to have interests (hobbies) that you fill your daily schedule with. In my opinion our interests establish part of who we are.

Computers & Technology

The majority of related topics in this field are of interest to me because of my studies and work. Of course it's a really broad spectrum and you can’t learn everything. Web development and data science are the two main fields that interest me the most. Web development is my every day job, and therefore I have more experience and familiarity in this area. Data science on the other hand is a pleasant activity. I'm following online courses and getting my hands dirty with small projects for now. However gaining experience and working in this area is a near future goal. In general learning about new technologies and frameworks is always enjoyable. Actually anything that raises the question word “how?” is worth researching. And this is a general approach I follow.

Arts & Entertainment

I know that I’m far from artistic as a character. However, exploring art's diversity is a nice challenge even if you suck at it. Visiting museums, galleries and other cultural affairs usually gives a feeling of calmness and ease of mind. Movies are another interesting sector of the artistic field. Lately Im really into old fashioned movies from the the period of 30s-70s. The simplicity, playfulness and originality in the dialogue, the beauty in the old classy style of living and human interaction is what makes old movies incomparable. My attempt to start reading more books is not very successful. However if you do have any good recommendations, especially psychology ones, please drop me a message.

Travel

A major benefit and a reason I love traveling is that it broadens your horizons. Experiencing multiculturalism and diversity, out of your comfort zone, surely enlightens your view of the world. Meeting people from all over the world and hearing their stories is another beneficial aspect of it. Even though I have travelled only around Europe so far, it's definitely on my bucket list to visit other continents as well.

Crafts

DIY projects can benefit one in many ways. They require a combination of manual work and creativity, and you also keep your brain sharp. It’s a great way to acquire new skills and the satisfaction you get from achieving something yourself is incomparable. So I always do a first attempt to build or repair something, around the house, myself, before seeking help. Other DIY projects that I enjoy relate to interior design and uplifting psychology.

Sports & Fitness

"Νους υγiης εν σώματι υγιεί" which translates to "a healthy mind in a healthy body". Exercising is always a nice break from your daily routine. Besides all the health benefits, it is proven to improve your overall state. And this assists in the continuation of a productive and inventive day. My program usually consists of home workouts, occasionally hitting the gym and outdoor activities. Thankfully in the Netherlands, like it or not, there is always a minimum amount of exercise because of the daily cycling.
